Welcome to Daisy Resort


Perfectly located in an outstanding hillside position with stunning views of Hoang Lien Son mountains, moss-green rice terrace plots and the poetic Muong Hoa valley, the five-star Pao’s Sapa Leisure Hotel is proud to possess the most premium rooms in the beautiful Sapa town.


Owned and operated by CTX Holdings, Pao’s Sapa Leisure Hotel offers 223 rooms featuring the cultural identity of Sapa, subtly blended with high-class rooms and impeccable facilities. Boasting charming curves in harmony with its natural surroundings, Pao’s Sapa Leisure Hotel is unquestionably a fabulous destination for a visit that will awaken your senses.
